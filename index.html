    🗺 Navigation
    How do I explore the map?
    Click and drag to pan, scroll to zoom. Click any region card in the bottom bar to jump to it.
    What is Story Mode?
    Press ▶ Start Story Mode to automatically travel through historical scenes in sequence. Use ⏸ Pause and ✕ Exit to control playback.
    What does "Take Me Somewhere, Sometime" do?
    Jumps to a random historical scene from the entire database.
    ★ Bookmarks
    How do I bookmark a scene?
    Tap the small ★ star in the top-right corner of any episode card. A gold star means it's bookmarked; tap again to remove it.
    Where do I find my bookmarks?
    Open 🎬 My Playlists in the bottom bar and switch to the ★ Bookmarks tab to see all your saved scenes grouped by region.
    📋 Playlists
    How do I create a playlist?
    Click 🎬 My Playlists in the bottom bar, press + to create one, then search and add scenes.
    Can I choose music for my playlist?
    Yes — inside the playlist editor click 🎵 Music to pick a track, or leave it on Auto to match the region.
    ⚙ Settings
    How do I change the colour theme?
    In the General tab, pick a colour swatch under Appearance → Colour Theme.
    Where are my settings saved?
    If you're signed in, all settings sync automatically to your account across devices. In guest mode, settings are saved in this browser's local storage.
    How do I reset everything?
    Go to General → Data and click ↺ Reset Defaults.
    🎵 Audio
    Music doesn't play on first load?
    Browsers require a user interaction before audio can start. Click anywhere and music will begin on the next scene.
    Can I mute during story mode?
    Yes — click the 🔕 Mute button in the top-right, or set BGM Volume to 0 in Settings.
    📬 About
    What version is this?
    World History Documentary — an interactive single-page history explorer. See 📚 Sources for data credits.
    👤 Account
    Do I need an account?
    No — you can use Guest Mode and everything is saved locally in your browser. Sign in to sync your playlists, bookmarks and settings across devices.
    How do I sign in or create an account?
    Click the 👤 Sign In button at the top left, or go to ⚙ Settings → Account. You can sign in or create a new account from there.
    Can I transfer my guest data to an account?
    Yes — go to Settings → Account while in Guest Mode and click Import Local Data before signing in. This uploads your local playlists, bookmarks and settings to your account.
    How does sync work?
    When signed in, any change to playlists, bookmarks or settings is automatically pushed to the cloud within a few seconds. On a new device, just sign in and your data will load immediately.
    How do I sign out?
    Go to Settings → Account and click Sign Out. Your local data is kept; it just stops syncing until you sign in again.
