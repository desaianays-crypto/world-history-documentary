    🗺 Navigation
    How do I explore the map?
    Click and drag to pan, scroll to zoom. Use the sidebar to browse continents, countries, and seasons.
    What is Story Mode?
    Press ▶ Start Story Mode to automatically travel through historical scenes in sequence. Use ⏸ Pause and ✕ Exit in the control bar to manage playback.
    What does "Take Me Somewhere, Sometime" do?
    Jumps to a single random historical scene from the currently selected region (or the full database if at world level).
    ★ Bookmarks
    How do I bookmark a scene?
    While a scene is open in Story or Scene view, press ★ Bookmark in the info panel. The button turns gold when the scene is saved. Press it again to remove it.
    Where do I find my bookmarks?
    Open 🎬 My Playlists from the bottom bar and switch to the ★ Bookmarks tab.
    📋 Playlists
    How do I create a playlist?
    Open 🎬 My Playlists and tap the ＋ button. You can also add scenes directly from the Explore tab using the ＋ button on each result row.
    Can I choose music for my playlist?
    Yes — open the playlist editor and use the 🎵 Music dropdown to pick a track, or leave it on Auto to follow the scene's region.
    How do I share a playlist?
    Inside the playlist editor, tap 🔗 Share. Copy the generated URL and send it — anyone who opens it will be prompted to import the playlist.
    ⚙ Settings
    How do I change the colour theme?
    In the General tab, pick a colour swatch under Appearance → Colour Theme.
    Where are my settings saved?
    When signed in, settings sync automatically to your account. In guest mode they are saved to this browser's local storage only.
    How do I reset everything?
    Go to General → Data and click ↺ Reset Defaults.
    🎵 Audio
    Music doesn't play on first load?
    Browsers block audio until the user interacts with the page. Tap anywhere and music will start on the next scene transition.
    Can I mute during story mode?
    Yes — tap the mute icon in the control bar, or drag the BGM Volume slider in Settings → General to zero.
    👤 Account
    Do I need an account?
    No — Guest Mode works fully offline and saves data locally. Create an account to sync your playlists, bookmarks and settings across devices.
    How do I sign in or create an account?
    Go to Settings → Account and tap Sign In / Create Account.
    How does sync work?
    Any change to playlists, bookmarks or settings is pushed to the cloud automatically within a few seconds. Sign in on any device to load your data instantly.
    How do I sign out?
    Go to Settings → Account and tap Sign Out. Your local copy is kept; sync pauses until you sign in again.
    📬 About
    What is World History Documentary?
    An interactive single-page history explorer combining a live map, curated scene database, and auto-playing story mode. See 📚 Sources for data credits.
